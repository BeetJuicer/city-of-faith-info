Data Deletion

We respect your privacy and provide multiple options for managing and deleting your data from the City of Faith application. Choose the option that best fits your needs below.

Delete Your Account

If you have an account and would like to permanently delete it along with all associated data:

  1. Sign in to your City of Faith account
  2. Navigate to Settings
  3. Click "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm your decision when prompted

Delete Analytics Data

If you want to keep your account but delete all gathered analytics data:

  1. Sign in to your City of Faith account
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Click "Request Data Deletion"
  4. Your analytics data will be removed while keeping your account active
